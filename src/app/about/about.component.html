Our Story

Biryani Boyz was born from a passion for authentic South Indian cuisine and a desire to share the rich culinary traditions of our homeland with the St. Louis community.

Our founders, having grown up in the vibrant food culture of South India, wanted to bring the authentic taste of home to Missouri. Every recipe in our kitchen has been passed down through generations, ensuring that each dish tells a story of tradition, love, and culinary excellence.
